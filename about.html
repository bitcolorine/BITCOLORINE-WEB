About

Hi, I'm Luisa.

I'm a front end-web developer, post-librarian, a writer and a bookseller. I work to create simple, effective, accessible and easy to maintain websites for small businesses and entrepreneurs who don't want to feel overwhelmed with tech-speak but want to have a solid web presence.

I believe in: good code, owning your content, accessibility over features and being able to grow your website with your business. Would you like to know more?

I believe that a website is created to work for your business but we need to find out what your business actually needs.

A well coded website is like a well built house, but it doesn't need to be huge. The more rooms you add to a house, the more you have to clean it, furnish it, repair it and well, maintain it.

The more features you add to your site without really needing them, well, you guessed it: you will have to maintain. Your website should do a few things, but it should do them well:

  1. Bring you new clients? How? What do you want them to do?
  2. Be accessible to everybody: on their phones, tablets and computers.
  3. Be easy to update to keep your clients informed and engaged.

And if your business grows?

Your website should too.

Let's work together.
